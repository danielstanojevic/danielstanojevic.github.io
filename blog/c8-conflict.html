Conflict

July 19th, 2015

Discuss a time you had a conflict. What was it about? What happened? When working on a project with a few other people. Everyone had their own opinion about how to do something. What was the source of the conflict? Lack of communication. What basic emotions did you feel upon analyzing the conflict? frustration What actions did you take to resolve your conflict? I simply communicated my idea and the fact that I was ok with trying someone else's idea first. If you could go back, what would you do differently, if anything? I would possibly try to communicate my idea and position earlier. What did you learn from this experience? Conflict can be easily resolved with proper communication.
