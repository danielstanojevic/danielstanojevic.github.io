Thinking Style

June 4th, 2015

What's my thinking style? Abstract Sequential According to my thinking style, what's hard for me is having too little time to deal with a subject thoroughly. I find that especially true in DBC. However, I've realized that there are effective ways to improve results with proper time management such as time boxing. My learning style suggests that I prefer to follow a plan, which is true. Books are organized in this linear fashion, and I do find that I learn effectively following along in a book. I definitely have a growth mindset. I actually took part of the Learning to Learn course that was recommended as an optional resource. I intend to finish that course to improve my learning style in general.
